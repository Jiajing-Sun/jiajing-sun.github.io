This course introduces students to the field of **financial econometrics**, covering key models used to assess financial markets and risk. Topics include **asset pricing**, **volatility modeling**, and **portfolio theory**. I served as the **lead instructor**, providing lectures, assignments, and guidance on applying econometric models to real financial data.

**Hours**: 40 hours

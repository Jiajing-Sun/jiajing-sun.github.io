## Abstract

**“Econometrics and Time Series Methods: Theory, Applications, and R Implementation”** (First Author), in collaboration with **Professor Hong Yongmiao** and **Professor Oliver Linton**. This English-language textbook covers a range of topics, including regression models, univariate and multivariate time series, volatility modeling, non-parametric methods, machine learning, and applications to real financial and macroeconomic data. It includes **R code** and extensive examples, designed for both classroom teaching and self-study. The textbook also has an accompanying **[website](https://quantinar.com/course/1033/econometrics-and-time-series-methods-theory-applications-and-r-implementation)** and **[GitHub repository](https://github.com/QuantLet/Econometrics_R)** for additional resources, making it accessible for a global audience of students and researchers.

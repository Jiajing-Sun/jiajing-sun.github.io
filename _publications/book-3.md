## Abstract

**中文摘要**:  
《计量经济学与时间序列方法：理论、应用与R实现》系统涵盖了回归模型、单变量与多变量时间序列、波动率建模、非参数方法、机器学习等内容，并在金融计量经济学和宏观经济数据分析中提供了实际应用。全书注重通过**R编程**实现理论与方法，强调案例分析和实证计算。

**English Abstract**:  
**“Econometrics and Time Series Methods: Theory, Applications, and R Implementation”** covers a variety of topics including **regression models**, **time series analysis**, **volatility modeling**, and **machine learning**. The textbook emphasizes **R programming** for hands-on learning and provides practical applications in econometrics, with a focus on real financial and macroeconomic data.

The book offers a comprehensive approach to **econometrics** and **time series analysis**, while integrating practical tools for hands-on learning. The material is designed for **graduate students**, **advanced undergraduates**, and professionals seeking a comprehensive understanding of **econometrics** and **time series modeling**.

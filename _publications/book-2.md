## Abstract 

**中文摘要**:  
本书系统介绍了中国金融科技的发展与应用，涵盖了区块链、大数据、云计算、人工智能等核心技术，以及其在金融服务中的应用。全书围绕“技术—业务—监管”三条主线，深入讨论金融科技的基础理论、核心功能及其发展实践。重点探讨了数字支付、金融基础设施、数字货币、智能银行、风险管理等应用领域，并结合大量典型案例进行分析。

**English Abstract**:  
This textbook provides a systematic overview of the **development and applications of financial technology (FinTech)** in China, covering core technologies such as **blockchain**, **big data**, **cloud computing**, and **artificial intelligence (AI)**. The book also addresses key areas like **digital payments**, **digital currencies**, **financial infrastructure**, and **regulatory technology** in the context of **FinTech** applications.

The textbook is structured around three main themes: **Technology**, **Business**, and **Regulation**. It explores the theoretical foundations, core functions, and practical applications of **FinTech**. Special attention is given to **digital payment systems**, **financial infrastructure**, and **risk management** with real-world case studies from China and international contexts.
